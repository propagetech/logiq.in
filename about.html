ABOUT US

Home > About Us
Logiq was established in 2008 to cater to the industrial control and automation needs of customers in the automated people movement industry.

At the helm is a seasoned team of experienced technocrats who have the necessary experience and domain knowledge of the Industry in which the Company operates.

At Logiq, we endeavor to seamlessly convert concepts into quality products that can be mass-produced. Our ecosystem comprises of leading industrial designers, EMS vendors and partners in the sheet metal design and fabrication industry.

The aim of the company is to establish leadership in this industry by providing reliable, technically superior and cost-effective solutions to customers in the domestic and export markets.

The values of innovation and customer delight drive our efforts. Even though we are relatively young in the market - our performance records set us apart from the crowd.

We are at heart innovative. This drives us to harness knowledge of emerging and future technologies that impact the market space we operate in. We are well versed with wireless technologies that encompass RFID and NFC as well as Bluetooth and BLE and seek to apply them to the Elevator, Escalator and Automatic Sliding Door Market.

Our in-depth knowledge base and experience in high-speed design have propelled our TFT VGA Graphic Displays and Elevator Infotainment Systems.
