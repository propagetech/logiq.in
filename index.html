WELCOME TO LOGIQ EMBEDDED SYSTEMS

Logiq Embedded Systems India Pvt. Ltd. is a business venture jointly promoted by a seasoned team of technocrats and professionals with over three decades of experience in the building industry and an existing technology company with a proven track-record in the field of Electronics.

The company derives its strength from the combined skill-sets of its team members in the areas of Electronics, Mechanical Engineering, Management and Finance. Their experience in the identification and implementation of new technologies holds the company in good stead. The team of experienced professional managers and designers focus on innovation to deliver superior products for the delight our customers.

The establishment of the company was but a natural extension of the Promoters’ vision to bring to the ultimate customer the fruits of latest technology in the realm of Electronics as applied to Elevators/Escalators and Automatic Sliding Doors through the used RFID, Bluetooth, Biometric and Capacitive Sensing technologies. Expertise in High Speed design allows the company the flexibility to use High Speed ARM Processors and Graphic accelerators to deliver video and media entertainment products that harness cutting-edge technology.

The deep knowledge of these technologies and a very diversified ecosystem of Print processing, EMS, Sheet Metal Design and Fabrication and efficient Supply Chain management enable the company to maintain an edge in the market. We create the entire portfolio – right from ideation to concept development and product creation.
