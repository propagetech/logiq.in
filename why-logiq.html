WHY US ?

Home > Why Logiq ?

We are a Design and Manufacturing Company that takes our work very seriously.

We work with our customers in the Elevator/Escalator and Automatic Sliding Doors Industry with the goals to help them identify technologies that work best for them and to help them solve their problems with cost-effective, quality solutions.

At Logiq, Good design is not an exception; it is the norm.

Here are a few reasons why you will like working with us.

1. Your Goals Are Our Goals

We offer a balance of pleasing aesthetics, logic, reliability and ease of use. We have a dedicated team of CAD engineers and designers who can turn your requirements into a working product within a short span of time. We offer you a well-developed ecological system that is tailored for seamless manufacturing in all aspects of the product – Electronics or Mechanical.

2. We’re Highly Organized

We understand that for us to create work that hits the mark we must have a clear understanding of the whole product, its use, intended installation environment and compliance requirements. We have clear communication channels to share the overall requirements to everyone involved so that the end product is just as desired and expected by the client.

3. We Understand Your Needs

We know that most people are not designers and may have difficulty translating their objectives into actionable design decisions. Hence we have multiple interactions to get a clear understanding of your strategies and objectives. These we then translate into the design that best suits your needs. In other words, we speak your language, so that you don’t have to learn ours.

4. We Go the Extra Mile

We are a relatively new team, but the team has vast experience. We have succeeded in establishing a reputation of excellence amongst the several OEM customers we service. Yes, we try harder and will go out of our way to ensure that our clients are left delighted and impressed. We walk the extra mile so that your journey is stressfree.
