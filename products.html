CAR OPERATING PANELS

Our car operating panels have stylish capactitive touchscreens that provide a highly appealing and informative interface in comparison to standard elevator mechanical push buttons. Offered in both polycarbonate and glass variants, we can customize the user interface panel and offer it in a variety of sizes upto 900 mm height and 400 mm width as per your requirements.

The polycarbonate fascia version is virtually tamper proof and supports a surface that is hard to break, dislodge or destroy.

Display versions include the traditional 7-Segment and Dot-Matrix displays as well as Custom LCD’s and TFT VGA Full Color Graphic displays.

RFID and Biometric Readers can be integrated in the COP.

The COP supports a COB-based Emergency Lamp as well. The illumination level is maintained as per Lift Codes in force.

Custom floor designations such a -1, G, L, P1, P2 can be provided with quick turnaround times. The front fascia can also be branded.

The touch technology was developed to comply with the elevator environment whilst maintaining an elegant flush mounted panel sensitive enough to respond to a gloved touch.

The COP also caters to vision impaired patrons through its ability to accept commands from a conventional button that can wirelessly communicate with the Car Operating Panel for floor selection.

The COP accepts parallel and serial interface connections.

LANDING OPERATING PANELS

The Landing Operating Panels are offered to complement the look and feel of the Car Operating Panels. Likewise, they too are offered in polycarbonate and glass versions.

The capactitive touchscreen solution provides a highly appealing and informative interface in comparison to standard elevator mechanical push buttons and render the touch-buttons vandal resistant.

The standard LOP is offered with 7-Segment, 14-Segment, Dot Matrix and Custom LCD displays.

The LOP accepts standard gray code input and can also be offered in the serial communication mode. Operation with proprietary protocols may require an additional protocol convertor.

ELEVATOR CONTROLLER

The AS380 Series integrated drive controllers are modernistic elevator-specific integrated control and drive units that have safety and reliability of the elevators’ motor drive circuit at the core of its design philosophy.

The Integrated Drive Controllers are offered in both 5.5 KW and 11 KW Versions.

Salient features include redundancy for the control processor as well as the drive processor, various communication options as well as parallel control interfaces, advanced group control that supports up to 8 Elevators in a group and a Vector Control that adjusts motor speed smoothly to deliver an exceptional level of comfort during start and stop operations, while the PWM dead band compensation reduces motor noise.

It adapts to synchronous as well as asynchronous motors.

ELEVATOR ILLUMINATION KITS.

The Elevator Lighting Kits have been engineered specifically for elevator applications.

With optics that have been optimized for use with elevators, these kits provide premium lighting as per lift codes in force at 1/8th the energy levels of their halogen counterparts.

The drive unit is designed to accept an auxiliary input from an emergency power source and provide a bump-less transfer from raw power to emergency power and vice-versa.
